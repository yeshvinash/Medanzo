ABOUT US

MedanzoTM Healthcare Solutions better understands the challenges in the industry of medical data management, including Medical Data Curation, Abstraction of Medical Charts, Electronic Health Records, Oncology Data, Adverse Event Data, and Biomarker Data, along with Medical Chart Review and Medical Records Retrieval.

At MedanzoTM Healthcare Solutions, we provide the best solutions for the aforementioned services through our unique quality assurance,timeline management, data privacy, and HIPPA compliance, which makes us different from other similar industries.

Our leadership team includes experts from various specialties like oncology, cardiology, orthopaedics, ophthalmology, gynaecology, pharmacology, microbiology, and others. The core team consists of doctors, physicians, dentists, qualified nurses, pharmacists, etc. Under the expert leadership, our team along with wide experience within the industry is committed towards complete client satisfaction for the medical data services.

We have an obligation to challenge the legacy, to enable new levels of precision, insight, and outcomes. We aspire to attract, develop, motivate, inspire, and retain top-notch and passionate talent.

The mission of MedanzoTM Healthcare Solutions is to accelerate insights and improve outcomes for patients through scientific expertise and real-world data in partnership with the leading biomedical innovators, healthcare providers, and medical societies.
